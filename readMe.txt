
Finding the best possible combination from 7 different cards from a deck of cards in poker.

In Poker there are 10 different rankings of hands:
1.Royal flush
2.Straight flush
3.Four of a kind
4.Full house
5.Flush
6.Straight
7.Three of a kind
8.Two pair
9.One pair
10.High card

This project aims to find the best algorithm to finding the best hands out of 7 different cards
